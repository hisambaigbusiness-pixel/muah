Meri Pyari Taibu

for Jasmine, from Hisam

I know you are a bit sad, and you must be feeling this way after what happened to you yesterday, especially with your mom and how she reacted. It must have felt like she didn't trust you enough when she asked to see your phone, and that feeling of distrust from someone like a mother, who we trust the most in this world, can make our hearts feel awful and heavy. I might be wrong about this, and it may be something else, but either way I hope you were able to talk to her again, I wanted to make you feel heard. It's the least I can do for you, along with other things that have been on my mind and heart for quite some time, my Taibu.

Sometimes you might wonder why I told my family, and I realize that because of me, you changed a lot in your life when you didn't need to, as your mom will never see you the same way again, and there will be a weird energy for a while. In our culture, as much freedom as boys have in these matters, girls have a bit less, whether we accept it or not, and I understand that. And somewhere along the way, you compromised your peace of mind by accepting me into your life, and even more so by telling your mom about me this early. I'm glad you felt comfortable enough to share our relationship with your mom and stand by me, especially when you knew they would never expect anything like this from you. Doing this is no small thing, so whether anyone understands you or not, I understand you, and how strong you are as a person in every way.

I love you for how clear you've always been, you never left me guessing, and you gave me such clarity that it felt easy. I guess that's what it's supposed to feel like to love someone. And the way you never let this relationship be affected by outside circumstances, every single time you go through something, you still put in the effort to keep this going, it puts a smile on my face every time and shows how emotionally intelligent you are. It shows me clearly how serious you are about us. A lot of times I feel like I've fallen behind in leading you the way I should as a responsible partner. I feel terrible about the times I failed to lead and protect you the way I could have. Sometimes I reacted emotionally and made you feel sad. I never felt at peace after those arguments, they never felt worth it, even when my arguments felt logical or reasonable to me. I always felt awful afterward, realizing I was expecting you to think the way I do. So I'm sorry for those moments.

I've learned not to ask anything for myself, except to make dua that I become capable of holding you in the future, and that I'm given the strength to withstand whatever life throws at me, so that I never hurt you, my Jasmine, and that one day we become people our kids will look up to.

Before going to sleep, I was feeling a bit uncertain about what I could do to support you emotionally in situations like yesterday, when I'm away from you, and I thought of writing this letter for you. I know I've written similar ones before, but today I wanted to express my gratitude for having you in my life. You made me believe that love doesn't have to be conditional. All my life, I've felt loved for what I've done and how I've performed. I can never say my parents didn't love me, they did, they did what they knew... so I can never blame them, and I'm always grateful for my family. Although my mum knows me more than anyone and has loved me, you're someone who made me feel worthy of empathy without any condition. You gave me your affection and love when you didn't have to, and that made me rethink a lot about life.

There are always times when I'm surrounded by family, friends, and siblings, but still feel unheard and unseen, regardless of the sleepless nights and things I gave up to man up and get my family out of the hole we were in, at a time similarly aged guys were playing games. I'm grateful for that, and I don't expect anything in return from my family, but sometimes I'm only human, and the least I hope for is some genuine affection or appreciation for what I'm doing — not saying they aren't grateful, but they never try to ask or know what's happening with me, how I'm feeling. But it's okay, I don't need it to get by. I've never received gifts or expressions of love from anyone before, and I don't need them either — for others. You are enough for me, so thank you for every single gift you've given me. Out of all of them, I love you the most, my love. Feeling loved, or expressing myself, was so unfamiliar that it used to feel strange to me, I used to say it's silly, haha, but you changed that and gave me a new perspective. And my love, I know these letters are never enough to show what's in my heart, which beats for you and only you, but I'm doing what I can. I hope this made you feel loved, even for a second, and I hope you felt the warmth of my heart and my intentions toward you. I'm trying to make the theme a bit more to your liking as well, with the blue rose and jasmine, I hope you like it.

And holy smokes, that was a lot of reading! I could write for days for you, but anyway, love you.

your loving,
Hisam
